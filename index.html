Selected Publication

Video-based Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification via Style Disturbance Defense and Dual Interaction. (MM’23, Oral)

  • Chuhao Zhou, Jinxing Li, Huafeng Li, Guangming Lu, Yong Xu, and Min Zhang.
  • Highlight.
    • The goal of this work is to learn more comprehensive sequential features that are invariant to both intra- and inter-modal discrepancies.
    • Style Attack and Defense Module (SAD) with Style Augmentation (SA): It models the intra- and inter-modal discrepancies as different types of image styles and guides the model to achieve the robustness towards both discrepancies from a novel style disturbance manner.
    • Graph-based Dual Interaction (GDI): It extends the sequential features learning from single video to multiple videos of the same identity, obtaining both cross-view and cross-modal complementary information.
  • [Paper Link]   [Code Link]

Multi-view Instance Attention Fusion Network for Classification

  • Jinxing Li✝, Chuhao Zhou✝, Xiaoqiang Ji, Mu Li, Guangming Lu, Yong Xu, and David Zhang. (✝ means ‘Equal Contribution’.)
  • Highlight.
    • The proposed MvIAFN not only exploits the instance-correlation across various views, but also achieves the deep representation for each instance. To the best of our knowledge, MvIAFN is the very first effort of jointly taking the IBL and deep learning method into a unified model for classification.
    • A novel selection algorithm is also attach to ensure that the selected template instances not only contain diverse intra-class information but are also noise-free.
